Built for people who manage real fleets

Not another dashboard you have to babysit. PrinterMonitor finds your printers, tracks what matters, and tells you before it becomes a ticket.

01 · supplies

Toner you can trust

Live CMYK levels per device, with predicted run-out dates from real usage trends. Order before you're empty, not after.

02 · discovery

Auto-discovery

Point the proxy at a subnet and printers appear on their own. No spreadsheets, no manual IP entry, no per-device setup.

03 · status

Status at a glance

Online, low, or offline — every printer, every site, on one board. Page counts and uptime tracked continuously.

04 · trends

History that answers questions

7, 30, or 90-day trends on usage and supplies. Spot the printer quietly burning through toner before finance asks why.

05 · networks

Every site, one view

Branch offices, warehouses, remote sites — monitor printers across any number of subnets from a single dashboard.

06 · privacy

Your data stays yours

The proxy runs on your network and talks out over encrypted TLS. Printer data never leaves your control unencrypted.
